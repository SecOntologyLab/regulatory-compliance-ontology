Cross-Domain Compliance Patterns

🔗Pattern 1: Shared Requirements

GDPR applies in BOTH civilian and defence contexts. Privacy requirements (R1, R3) share identical regulatory sources across domains.

🔀Pattern 2: Divergent Frameworks

Security requirements (R2, R4) use NIS2 for civilian deployments but NATO-specific controls (NIAP, NSP, CDP) for defence.

➕Pattern 3: Additive Compliance

Defence compliance is cumulative: all applicable civilian requirements PLUS NATO-specific obligations. Never substitutive.
